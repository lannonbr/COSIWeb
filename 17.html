    • Junior year in COSI

    These are some major contributions of mine have been made during my Junior year

    • COSI Ansible Playbooks (Summer 2016)

      I wrote an Ansible Playbook which automates the process of adding central Authentication (LDAP/Kerberos/NFS) to any Debian machine in the labs. I'll be working with James Bruska to continue automating various tasks in the labs as well as have a rich amount of documentation on how to use the scripts.

    • ML Course (Planned project)

      Michael Fulton and myself are planning on teaching a weekly course on the fundamentals of Machine Learning which people who take the course will be able to get credit for.

    • COSI Newsletter (Summer 2016 - Present)

      The weekly newsletter, also known as COSI News, is a project I begun to give a peek into the week of what is happening in COSI, and what progress has been made. So far it has consisted of upcoming projects, but as the semester begins, I plan on adding a link to the previous week's Meeting Minutes, a schedule of events for the upcoming week, and a "Meet a Cosinaught" section as well.

    • COSI History (Summer 2016 - Present)

      COSI has existed for over 15 years, and I wanted to create a page to look back through the history of the labs and try to get as much of a complete timeline of the labs as we can.

    • "Linux Workflow Workshop" Series (Planned Project)

      Michael Fulton and myself are also planning on being the main leaders of a series of workshops where we give introductions to many different aspects of the Linux Workflow (Text editors, Version Control, Testing & Continuous Integration, etc.). Michael and I are going to be the leads for the series, but anyone else is welcome to contribute to the project.

    • COSI VM (Planned Project)

      The COSI VM was a project suggested by Jeanna Matthews to give people who are new to Linux a VirtualBox image with a setup very similar to the lab images.
